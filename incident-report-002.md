# Incident Report 002

## Summary

A suspicious URL was reported by a user after receiving an email requesting account verification.

## Indicators

- URL: http://verify-account-security.com
- No HTTPS
- Suspicious keyword: verify
- Domain impersonation pattern

## Risk Level

High

## Investigation Findings

The URL contained multiple phishing indicators commonly used to deceive users into revealing sensitive information.

## Recommendations

- Block the domain
- Notify affected users
- Conduct phishing awareness training
- Monitor related email activity
